Client Centric Approach

We have developed a successful engagement model for Enterprise Architecture, closely aligned to the TOGAF Methodology and drawing upon industry frameworks such as a DMBOK and Lean Six Sigma. Our engagement with clients usually starts with the creation of a future state vision, focused on the strategy of the organisation, the trends and disruptions facing it, and the capabilities required. This work is future-oriented, innovative and requires visionary thinking.

With a target architecture agreed, it is then possible to look at the current architecture and to plan how we deliver your vision. The delivery of the agreed target architecture will close the gaps between where we are today and the future state. Our approach considers the holistic needs of the enterprise and comprises three key tenants - business, information, and technology.

Business

  • Define Business Architecture
  • Capability Modelling & Assessments
  • Process Design & Optimisation
  • EA Principles & Governance
  • Portfolio Management
  • Benefits Realisation

Information

  • Define Data Architecture
  • Analytics & Business Intelligence
  • Data Governance, Sharing & Security
  • Master Data Management
  • Taxonomies & Ontologies
  • Data Modelling

Technology

  • Define Technology Architecture
  • Strategy Creation & Communication
  • Roadmaps & Lifecycle Management
  • Application Rationalisation
  • Service Design & Improvement
  • License Review & Optimisation

Alto IT is unlike many of the larger IT consultancy practices - we pride ourselves on taking a more bespoke and pragmatic approach to EA. We will work closely with your Executive Committee or Project Sponsor, establishing ourselves as a core part of the transformation team and liaising with project stakeholders in order to ascertain the true priority of issues and helping to clearly articulate the drivers for change. This allows Alto IT to offer a flexible, virtual EA function, operating as a trusted partner alongside any in-house teams you may already have established.

Recent Projects

Image Capture & Big Data Processing

Working with a leading client in the mapping sector, we were tasked with the next-gen data storage and processing platform for high altitude arial imagery. We were responsible for defining the high-level architecture utilising technology components from ESRI and MS Azure, streamlining data flows and orchestration, and building a scalable service for the client's anticipated explosion in data storage and processing requirements.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Working with multiple clients in the Utility & Public Sector, we have supported the selection, procurement and implementation of RPA platforms from Blue Prism and Automation Anywhere. Our engagement in RPA projects has previously included defining enterprise-wide Robotic Process Automation strategy; defining approach, identifying opportunities for automation, and standing up infrastructure to create Robotics Centre of Excellence.

Data Strategy, Analytics & BI

Working with clients to provide Data Architecture expertise, with responsibilities commonly including ownership for defining the vision, strategy, roadmaps, reference architectures, design patterns & governance frameworks to enable clients to exploit their data and become data drive in future decision making. We often work with client to implement modern data stacks built around Microsoft Azure technologies including Data Lake, Data Factory, Azure SQL, Cosmos DB, Power BI etc.

Digital Transformation

Working with clients to identify and explore areas for digital innovation, providing architectural guidance and hosting ‘art of the possible’ workshops to drive enterprise-wide improvements covering business process redesign, data exploitation, cloud adoption, cost rationalisation, Robotics and AI/ML, Office365 utilisation, SharePoint, Power BI & Azure Analytics.
